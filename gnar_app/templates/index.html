Quick Start

If you haven't already, start by making an account.

Scoring

Attend a workout or meeting: 100 points

Win a game at a bi-weekly meeting: 500 points

Post a climb and have at least one other climber complete it: 250 points (limit 15/month)

Do a climb that someone else posted: 100 points

Beta so weird a stranger comments on it: 250 points

Have breakfast in front of CRG and be first in: 500 points

Give a snack to a teammate in need and tell them "it looks like you need this": 250 points (limit 2/month)
